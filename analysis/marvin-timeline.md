# Timeline of Events According to Marvin Heemeyer

Source scope: this chronology summarizes events as Marvin describes them in the tape transcripts. It is not an independent fact-check.

Method note: this timeline intentionally steel-mans Marvin's grievance narrative. Where dates are vague, contradictory, or only relative, items are placed in the best-guess chronological order most favorable to his argument, while avoiding claims he does not make. In practice, that means ambiguous events are ordered to preserve Marvin's strongest causal chain: local plans predate him; his auction purchase disrupts those plans; water and sewer access is blocked; the concrete plant process revives the earlier plan; town/legal/media actors fail to protect him; he concludes that normal remedies are closed.
